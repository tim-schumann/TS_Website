about me


„Enthusiastic in combining art, technology and science to create something more. Currently, focussing on innovations such as artificial intelligence, the metaverse and digital fabrication strategies and seeking implementations in the industry. Also I like to experiment with biomaterials and sustainable design strategies.

After gaining my Bachelor in Architecture at RWTH Aachen I worked at gmp architects and LRO architects in several BIM- and design projects. In my master's at TU Delft I specialized in computational design and digital manufacturing. Since 2023, I am working at digitales bauen - Part of Drees & Sommer on improving design workflows, implementing modular design into projects and realizing parametric structures.
